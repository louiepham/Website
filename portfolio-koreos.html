About

Koreos is a K-Pop dance cover team at UCLA dedicated to the development and education of the art of dance. We aspire to strengthen friendships through the mutual love for K-Pop as well as spread awareness of Korean culture and its significance. Our team actively interacts with our viewers to promote the Hallyuwave, encouraging them to also participate in sharing our mutual passion and interest for Korean Culture

As their current Artistic Director, not only do I solidify their visual identity, but also further develop their content to branch into different fields.

In addition to art direction, I also film and edit a majority of the videos. I teach and lead a team of dancers from the organization to become editors to help with the immense content we produce. Not only did I streamline the editing process, but I also learned how to lead and work with a group of people.

Target Market

The ideal consumer of our products is the millennial K-pop fan who is interested in dancing and creating bonds with other K-pop fans. Because our demographic is the same as K-pop’s, I found it to be 85% female with the largest number of fans within the age 18-24. However, there is also a lot of young K-pop fans between the ages of 14-18. I created a survey to interview our fans to not only understand their needs, but also create entertainment for them that they did not realize they’d enjoyed. Through this research, I was able to create a user persona.

To help this channel grow further, I have conducted research to look further into their demographic and see what types of videos the fanbase is interested in. Thus, I developed Koreos Variety and advised them to also produce more dance tutorials to expand their fanbase and demographic.

I used colors as a visual language to help viewers identify the variety of content this channel produces. These colors allow the viewer to visually navigate to a specific type of content they are interested in, enhancing the UX on Koreos' YouTube Channel.

A series of stationary format was created for the brand.

Invoices, media kits, and legal documents to validate their professional identiy with other businesses.

Furthermore, I actively participate in connecting with branded content and sponsors to help fund this organization.
