Cognition

December, 2013

Each story was related to a certain region of the brain, and this interactive poster series allows the viewer to flip the page to reveal the whole image.

Taught by Noa Kaplan, the class explores the association between concept and image creation. The posters were based on the following short stories:

The Aleph, Jorge Luis Borges
The Ones Who Walk Away from Omelas, Ursula Le Guin
And He Built a Crooked House, Robert A. Heinlein
The Bear Came Over the Mountain, Alice Munro
Sea Oak, George Saunders
The Distance of the Moon, Italo Calvino
He Who Shrank, Henry Hasse
